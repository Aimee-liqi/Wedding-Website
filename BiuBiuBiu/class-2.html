Pay attention those details


1.Invition


Invitations are the first impression of a new wedding, so the invitation is particularly important. When writing an invitation,you must write the name of the invited person, be sure to be formal, and cannot write nicknames. In addition,the date and location of the wedding should be written clear.If possible, you can attach a map bar to make sure guests to find the location.


2.Wedding usher


Because your wedding venue may unfamiliar to guests, they may be temporarily confused to find their own seats, so at this time a usher is particularly important. After all, having some ushers can avoid the guests' awkward position.Especially for some important guests,they must be led to important seats.


3.Wedding acknowledge


It is a necessary process for the end of the wedding, if your wedding is relatively small, then yo can thank the guests one by one.Howerer, if the amount of guests are more, people can prepare some intimate gifts in return, send to the guests at the wedding end, the guests will be able to feel your good intentions.
